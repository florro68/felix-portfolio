Felix I. Flores – Photography Portfolio

Hello and welcome to my site. I am Felix, a photographer, observer, and storyteller. This portfolio showcases my work across landscapes, wildlife, and seascapes. Each gallery holds a piece of my journey.

Wild Horses

Captured with patience and respect, these photographs reveal the quiet power and untamed spirit of wild horses in their natural environment.

UK Quiet

Still landscapes from the UK, focusing on mood, heritage, and the spaces between moments.

Waterfalls

Flow and form meet in these long-exposure studies of waterfalls, each with its own rhythm and voice.

Seascapes

Minimal, moody, and often silent — these are moments where sea and sky meet in quiet conversation.

Engure Seascape

From Latvia’s Engure port, these photographs capture Baltic stillness and maritime solitude.
